Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Ansora account and all associated data using either method below.

⚠ This action is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Your profile and personal information
  • All match connections and compatibility scores
  • All chat messages
  • All question answers
Option 1 — Delete in the app
  1. Open the Ansora app on your phone
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm in the popup — your account will be deleted immediately
or
Option 2 — Request via email

Send an email to quixentapps@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Ansora account" and include your registered phone number. We will process your request within 7 business days.
